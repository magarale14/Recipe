Reeta's Recipe

Ingredients needed to make lassi:

1) Get 1 3/4 cups of plain yogurt (Cabot Low Fat Plain Greek Style Yogurt 32 Oz.

2) 6 cubes ice,crushed

3) 1 1/2 cups ice water

4) 2 teaspoons white sugar

5) 1 pinch salt

Direction on how to make it:

In the blender, mix the yogurt,ice water, sugar and salt.

Put the ice cubes in tall glasses and pour the mixture.

Nutritional Facts:

Per Serving: 50 calories; 1.1g fat; 6.4 carbohydrates; 3.8g protein; 4mg cholesterol; 51mg sodium
